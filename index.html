FAQ

Does Yeetta Mon require an agent to be installed?
For some data yes, but we can provide many visualisations without agents on your local servers.
Can I have a local collector or does every agent/service send data to Yeetta Mon?
You can install a "Concentrator" at each of your sites, which in turn forwards all data to Yeetta Mon.
